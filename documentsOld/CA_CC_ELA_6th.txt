CA Common Core ELA 6th grade: [
    Reading Standards for Literature (RL):

Cite textual evidence to support analysis of what the text says explicitly and make inferences.
Determine a theme or central idea and analyze its development.
Analyze how a particular sentence, chapter, scene, or stanza fits into the overall structure of a text.
Determine the meaning of words and phrases as they are used in a text.
Analyze how a text's structure contributes to its meaning.
Reading Standards for Informational Text (RI):

Cite textual evidence to support analysis of what the text says explicitly and make inferences.
Determine the main idea of a text and analyze its development.
Analyze the interactions between individuals, events, and ideas in a text.
Determine the meaning of words and phrases as they are used in a text.
Compare and contrast the overall structure of events, ideas, concepts, or information in two or more texts.
Writing Standards (W):

Write arguments to support claims with clear reasons and evidence.
Write informative/explanatory texts to examine a topic and convey ideas and information.
Write narratives to develop real or imagined experiences or events.
Produce clear and coherent writing appropriate to the task, purpose, and audience.
Conduct research projects to answer specific questions or solve problems.
Speaking and Listening Standards (SL):

Engage effectively in a range of collaborative discussions with diverse partners.
Interpret information presented in diverse media and formats.
Present information, findings, and supporting evidence clearly and concisely.
Language Standards (L):

Demonstrate command of the conventions of standard English grammar and usage when writing or speaking.
Demonstrate command of the conventions of standard English capitalization, punctuation, and spelling.
Determine or clarify the meaning of unknown and multiple-meaning words and phrases.
